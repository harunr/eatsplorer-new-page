Chef Lapo’s Kitchen long table
experience in Observatory.

+ Included

Fynbos Wine-Pairing in the Experience Price.

A four-course, culinary feast enjoyed with fellow foodies at a long table in the comfort of an Italian chef’s quirky home? One who grew up learning the art of Tuscan cooking from his Mama, fell in love with Cape Town, and is super passionate about cooking seasonally and sustainably using only proudly South African produce? Now that’s amore.

Highlights

  • Hosted by chef Lapo Magni, winner of the Italian version of Masterchef “Land of the Cook”.
  • Join fellow foodies around the chef’s dining room table.
  • The opportunity to directly interact with the chef as he cooks and hosts.
  • Lapo talks you through the menu giving context to his culinary choices and sustainable food philosophy.
  • A celebration of seasonal, sustainable and ethical
    produce.

Overview

You might say Lapo Magni was born with a wooden spoon in his mouth. No, really. He grew up in Florence, Italy, where his mother, a trained chef, taught him the secret to cooking Tuscan food: putting your heart and soul into every plate.
